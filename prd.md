# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# Emmnez Water Solution Ltd Website

Version: 1.0
Status: Ready for Design & Development
Platform: Marketing Website
Priority: Mobile First

---

# Overview

The Emmnez Water Solution website will serve as the company's primary digital presence and lead-generation platform.

The website should communicate trust, professionalism, simplicity, and expertise in water treatment and engineering solutions.

Unlike traditional water engineering websites that focus heavily on machinery and technical jargon, the Emmnez website should make water solutions feel simple, accessible, and reliable.

The website should help visitors quickly understand:

* What Emmnez does
* Why they should trust Emmnez
* How to contact Emmnez

The entire experience should feel clean, modern, and premium without being complex.

---

# Product Vision

To create the most trusted and user-friendly water solutions website in Nigeria.

The website should position Emmnez as a reliable partner for clean water solutions while maintaining a modern, premium brand image.

---

# Design Principles

## Simplicity First

Every section should have a clear purpose.

No unnecessary features.

No clutter.

No overwhelming technical information.

---

## Mobile First

The majority of users will discover and browse the website from mobile devices.

All design decisions must prioritize mobile usability before desktop layouts.

Desktop layouts should expand naturally from mobile designs.

---

## Trust Through Clarity

Visitors should never be confused about:

* Services offered
* Company credibility
* Contact information
* Next steps

---

## Premium Through Restraint

The website should feel premium through:

* Excellent typography
* Professional photography
* Generous spacing
* Clean layouts
* Smooth interactions

Not through excessive animations or visual effects.

# Photography Guidelines

Use:

* Real project installations
* Real equipment
* Real technicians
* Real customer environments

Avoid:

* Generic stock imagery
* Water splash graphics
* Overused corporate imagery

Authenticity should be prioritized.

---

# Animation Guidelines

Allowed:

* Fade-ins
* Smooth scrolling
* Subtle hover effects

Avoid:

* Excessive motion
* Auto-playing distractions
* Heavy animations

The website should feel calm and professional.
