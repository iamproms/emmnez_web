About Emmnez Water

Dedicated to providing clean, safe, and reliable water solutions across Nigeria.

Who We Are

Emmnez Water Solution was established on 12 June 2015 as a Water Engineering, purification, installation of water factories, and consulting practice.

Due to the changing built environment and the growing needs of our clients, it became necessary to broaden the services provided by Emmnez. We offer multi-disciplinary consulting services in areas associated with achieving clean and healthy drinking water, sales of sachet water machines, bottling lines, machine accessories, treatment plants, and machine spare parts under one banner.

Emmnez Water Solution is registered specifically to deal with the purification and installation of water projects in diverse places where that service is required, such as hospitals, churches, schools, factories, and residential areas.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to build long term relationships with our customers and clients and provide exceptional customer services by pursuing business through innovation and advanced technology.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to provide quality services that exceed the expectations of our esteemed customers. Whether it is an installation project, water engineering project or sales of accessories, parts and machines, Emmnez is a leading practice that will exceed expectations and set new standards!

Ownership & Management

The company is in a 100% procurement recognition level and is headed by a dynamic, motivated, and efficient team currently led by our Managing Director, Mr. Emmanuel Ezebuiro (Tech Eng), assisted by Mrs. Emmanuel Ezebuiro and their body of staff.

Our Approach

Emmnez sees teamwork on every project as the key success ingredient and is responsible for creating this environment. Teams are built by combining the strengths of the individual members and refining skills to meet and exceed the Client’s expectations.

We believe in a hands-on approach on all our projects. The overall Manager is duty-bound in ensuring effective communication to all staff and at all levels. Emmnez is equipped with the latest technology and has the necessary staff and resources to ensure the best professional service is provided at all times.
